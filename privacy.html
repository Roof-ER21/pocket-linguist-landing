Privacy Policy

Nana's Design  ·  Pocket Linguist  ·  Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Pocket Linguist ("the App") is developed and operated by Nana's Design. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. We are committed to handling your information transparently and responsibly.

By using Pocket Linguist, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide the App's core functionality:

  • Usage data — screens viewed, features used, session length, and crash reports, collected anonymously through analytics.
  • Device information — device type, operating system version, and language settings, used solely for compatibility and performance optimization.
  • In-app progress — your learning progress, quiz scores, and language preferences, stored locally on your device.
  • Voluntary information — if you contact us by email, we retain that correspondence to respond to your inquiry.

We do not collect your name, address, payment information, or any sensitive personal data through the App itself.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Deliver and improve the App's language learning features
  • Diagnose technical issues and fix bugs
  • Understand aggregate usage patterns to prioritize new content
  • Respond to support requests you initiate

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Pocket Linguist integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Pinterest API — used to surface language-learning content and allow optional content sharing. Pinterest may collect data in accordance with the Pinterest Privacy Policy.
  • Analytics — we use anonymous, aggregated analytics (no personally identifiable information) to understand how users interact with the App and improve the experience.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — distribution platforms that may collect their own usage metrics per their respective privacy policies.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand how they handle your data.

Cookies and Tracking

The App itself does not use cookies. If you visit our website or associated web pages, standard web analytics may set cookies to measure traffic. These cookies contain no personally identifiable information and can be blocked through your browser settings without affecting your use of the App.

Data Retention

Learning progress and preferences are stored locally on your device and are deleted when you uninstall the App. Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 24 months in aggregate form, after which it is deleted or fully anonymized.

Support correspondence sent via email is retained for up to 12 months from the date of last contact, then deleted.

Children's Privacy

Pocket Linguist is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below. We will respond within 30 days.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data we process. Because most user data is stored locally on your device, you are also in direct control of its security through your device's own security settings (passcode, biometrics, encryption).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
